# 6. User Email Verification

## Scope

Unverified email handling and activation email resend.

## Preconditions

- Tester is logged out.
- Email/password account with unverified Firebase email exists.

## Scenario 6.1: Login with unverified email

- Login with the unverified email and correct password.
- App should not finish as a verified session.
- App should route to **Email Validation**.
- Screen should indicate email verification is required.
- Account email should be displayed.
- Resend action should be available.

## Scenario 6.2: Resend activation email

- Tap the resend action on **Email Validation**.
- New activation link should be requested.
- App should route to **Registration Verification**.
- Verification waiting screen should be shown.

## Scenario 6.3: Close verification flow

- Tap close/back on email validation or verification waiting screen.
- App should dismiss to **Home**.
- User should remain unauthenticated until email is verified.
