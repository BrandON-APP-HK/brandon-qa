# 16. Brand Follow Find Store

## Scope

Follow/unfollow brand and find-store navigation.

## Preconditions

- Brand campaign exists with active store locations.
- Test as guest and logged-in user.

## Scenario 16.1: Follow while logged out

- Start logged out.
- Open a brand campaign page.
- Tap follow button.
- Login bottom sheet should open.
- Follow state should not silently change before login.

## Scenario 16.2: Follow and unfollow while logged in

- Log in.
- Open a brand campaign page.
- Tap **Follow the brand**.
- Notification permission/token registration may be requested.
- Brand should become followed.
- Follow label/icon should update.
- Tap followed button again.
- Brand should become unfollowed.
- Label/icon should return to unfollowed state.

## Scenario 16.3: Sticky follow bar

- Open brand page.
- Scroll down.
- Bottom follow bar should appear/fade in as configured.
- Bottom follow button should match the main follow state.
- Tap bottom follow button.
- Same brand follow/unfollow behavior should occur.

## Scenario 16.4: Find store guarded

- Start logged out.
- Open brand page.
- Tap **Find a store**.
- Login bottom sheet should open.
- Map should not open.

## Scenario 16.5: Find store while logged in

- Log in.
- Open brand page.
- Tap **Find a store**.
- **Map** should open with campaign context.
- Header should show **Find a store**.
- Tap list icon.
- **Find Store List** should open.
- Select/close as available.
- App should preserve campaign context and return to brand page on close.
